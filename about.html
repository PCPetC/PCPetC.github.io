About

Hello! My name is Peter Chang, and I am a Graphic Design B.A. and Physics B.S., with a large amount of programming experience. I enjoy making art, conducting lab experiments, taking things apart and putting them back together, relaxing with videogames, and ofcourse, playing with my dogs. I have a big growth mindset and seem to always "bite off more than I can chew" in terms of taking classes, but that's part of how I learn. I want to create and invent many things, but I require a lot more knowledge before I am ready to do so.
